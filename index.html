Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

A spiritual journey through India's holiest cities

Day 1: Arrival in Ayodhya - The Birthplace of Lord Rama

Arrive in Ayodhya (via train/road/flight) and check-in to your hotel. After refreshment, begin your spiritual journey in this sacred city where Lord Rama was born.

Today's Highlights:
  • Shri Ram Janmabhoomi - The sacred birthplace of Lord Rama
  • Hanuman Garhi - Iconic temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman
  • Kanak Bhawan - Famous for its golden idols of Rama and Sita
  • Dashrath Mahal - Ancient palace of King Dashrath
  • Guptar Ghat - Evening visit to this sacred bathing spot
  • Saryu River Aarti - Mesmerizing evening ceremony
  • Surya Kund Laser Show - Spectacular light and sound show

Overnight: Ayodhya | Meals: Dinner

Day 2: Ayodhya to Prayagraj (Approx. 170 km / 4-5 hrs)

Early morning departure after breakfast. Enroute stop for refreshments before reaching the holy city of Prayagraj, home to the sacred Triveni Sangam.

Today's Highlights:
  • Triveni Sangam - Sacred confluence of Ganga, Yamuna and Saraswati rivers (Holy Dip)
  • Hanuman Mandir (Lete Hanuman) - Unique temple with reclining Hanuman idol
  • Anand Bhawan - Historic Nehru family residence turned museum
  • Alopi Devi Mandir - Mysterious shrine where Goddess disappeared
  • Evening Aarti at Sangam - Spiritual ceremony by the river

Overnight: Prayagraj | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

Day 3: Prayagraj to Chitrakoot (Approx. 130 km / 3-4 hrs)

Depart after breakfast to Chitrakoot, the place where Lord Rama spent 11 years of his exile. This tranquil town is nestled in the Vindhya mountain range.

Today's Highlights:
  • Ram Ghat - Sacred bathing spot on Mandakini River
  • Kamadgiri Parikrama - Circumambulation of the holy hill
  • Hanuman Dhara - Temple with natural water stream falling on Hanuman idol
  • Sphatik Shila - Rock where Sita left her footprint
  • Gupt Godavari - Mysterious caves with natural throne-like formations

Overnight: Chitrakoot | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

Day 4: Chitrakoot to Varanasi (Approx. 260 km / 6-7 hrs)

Early morning drive to Varanasi, the spiritual capital of India. Check-in and rest before afternoon sightseeing in this ancient city that's considered the holiest in Hinduism.

Today's Highlights:
  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple - One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva
  • Annapurna Devi Temple - Goddess of nourishment
  • Dashashwamedh Ghat - Most famous ghat in Varanasi
  • Evening Ganga Aarti - Spectacular fire ceremony on the ghats

Overnight: Varanasi | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

Day 5: Varanasi Sightseeing & Departure

Begin your final day with a spiritual boat ride on the sacred Ganges River at sunrise, witnessing the city come alive with morning rituals.

Today's Highlights:
  • Morning Boat Ride - Witness rituals and ceremonies along the ghats
  • Sarnath - Where Buddha gave his first sermon (Dhamek Stupa, Museum)
  • Tulsi Manas Temple - Beautiful temple with Ramcharitmanas inscriptions
  • Bharat Mata Temple - Unique temple dedicated to Mother India

After lunch, transfer to Railway Station / Airport for your return journey with divine blessings.

Meals: Breakfast
